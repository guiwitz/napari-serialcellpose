The CC0 data in the data_multiscene folder are from the Broad Bioimage Benchmark Collection BBBC047 dataset available on [IDR](https://idr.openmicroscopy.org/webclient/?show=screen-1251). Specifically, they from Plate ID: 6208, wells 1342082 for H15 and 1341790 for H23. The six fields of view were assembled in single multi-scene images using [bioio-ome-tiff](https://github.com/bioio-devs/bioio-ome-tiff).
